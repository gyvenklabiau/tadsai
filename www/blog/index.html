Time

24 hour cycle can be evenly divided into 2, 4, 6, 8, 12 or even 24 segments.

Doing so creates time intervals. For example spliting it into 4 they can be described by the movement of the arrow.

From 3 to 9 the arrow is moving down, then 9-15 to the left, then 15-21 up and 21-3 to the right.

Each of these periods represents 90 degrees of the circle and together they make up 360.

Each part of 24 cycle that is divided into 4 is 6 hours long. Or 360 minutes.

In that case a 1 degree of 24 hour clock thus represents 4 minutes.
